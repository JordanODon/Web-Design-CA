About Cars-R-Us

Meet the Founders

Welcome to Cars-R-Us, a quirky and unconventional electric vehicle (EV) distributor serving the Irish market. Our story began in a humble garage in Clondalkin, where our three fearless founders, Jordan, John, and Brendan, embarked on a daring mission to revolutionize the world of electric transportation.

Our Mission

Armed with nothing but a small loan of a million euros and a shared passion for clean energy, our founders transformed their garage into a bustling EV empire. Drawing inspiration from the EU Parliament's plan to phase out petrol and diesel vehicles by 2035, Jordan, John, and Brendan dedicated themselves to promoting carbon reduction plans and advancing the cause of environmental sustainability.

Our Range

Today, Cars-R-Us boasts a diverse selection of electric vehicles, including two distinct models with a myriad of customizable options. Our mission is to cater to the unique preferences and driving requirements of our customers, whether they're seeking the latest cutting-edge technology or a budget-friendly alternative. At Cars-R-Us, our team of enthusiastic professionals is committed to helping you find the perfect electric vehicle to fit your lifestyle and support a greener future.

Delivering Excellence

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our product offerings; we strive to create an unparalleled customer experience by streamlining every aspect of the EV acquisition process. From providing comprehensive resources to facilitating seamless communication, our goal is to make your journey with Cars-R-Us as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

Seamless Communication

Our state-of-the-art website, serves as a comprehensive hub for all your EV needs. Featuring an intuitive interface and a wealth of information, our platform allows you to effortlessly browse our selection of vehicles, compare specifications, and customize your ideal EV. Additionally, our dedicated customer service team is readily available to address any questions or concerns you may have throughout the process.

Market Potential

As the global shift towards sustainable transportation accelerates, the market potential for electric vehicles continues to soar. According to the International Energy Agency (IEA), the sales share of new electric vehicles in the EU has experienced remarkable growth, increasing from 3.2% in 2019 to 10% in 2020. This upward trend reflects a growing consumer demand for eco-friendly alternatives and presents a unique opportunity for Cars-R-Us to cement its position as an industry leader.

By continuously expanding our product offerings and refining our services, we aim to lead the charge in making electric vehicles the preferred choice for drivers across Ireland. As we embark on this exciting journey, we invite you to join us in embracing a cleaner, greener future for the automotive industry. Our commitment to staying at the forefront of emerging technologies and market trends ensures that we can provide our customers with the most innovative and efficient electric vehicles available.

Furthermore, our dedication to promoting carbon reduction plans and following environmentally sustainable practices solidifies our role as a responsible corporate citizen. As the demand for electric vehicles continues to rise, Cars-R-Us remains steadfast in its mission to facilitate the widespread adoption of eco-friendly transportation alternatives, ultimately contributing to a healthier planet for generations to come.
